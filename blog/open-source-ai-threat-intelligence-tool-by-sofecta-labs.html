Open Source AI Threat Intelligence Tool by Sofecta Labs

Empowering the community with our new open-source tool for automated threat intelligence gathering and LLM-powered summarization.

Giving Back to the Community

At Sofecta Labs, we believe in the power of collaboration and open-source software. That’s why we are excited to announce the release of our new AI-powered threat intelligence tool, designed to help organizations of all sizes better understand the threats they face.

Automated Intelligence Gathering

The tool automates the process of gathering intelligence from a variety of sources, including security blogs, RSS feeds, and GitHub repositories. This data is then processed and stored in an Elastic search cluster for easy analysis and visualization.

LLM-Powered Summarization

One of the tool's most powerful features is its ability to use LLMs to summarize complex security alerts and provide analysts with a clear and concise understanding of the threat. This saves valuable time and allows analysts to focus on the most critical issues.

"Open source is the foundation of modern cybersecurity, and we are proud to contribute our expertise to the community."

Empowering Analysts

By providing high-fidelity intelligence and automated summarization, our tool empowers security analysts to work more efficiently and effectively. We believe that this tool will be a valuable asset for any organization looking to improve its threat intelligence capabilities.
