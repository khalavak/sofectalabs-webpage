Sofecta Labs at Disobey 2025: A Weekend of Hacking, Learning, and Community

Recapping our experience at the biggest hacker convention in the Nordics.

The Hacking Community

Disobey is more than just a security conference—it’s a celebration of hacking culture and community. At Sofecta Labs, we are proud to be part of this community, and we had a fantastic time at this year's event.

Hacking and Learning

The weekend was filled with a wide range of activities, from workshops and talks to CTF competitions and networking opportunities. It was a great chance to learn from some of the best in the industry and share our own expertise.

Connecting with Others

One of the highlights of the weekend was connecting with other security professionals and enthusiasts. We had many interesting conversations and met many talented people who are passionate about cybersecurity.

"Disobey is a reminder of why we do what we do—to protect the digital world and build a stronger security community."

Looking Ahead to 2026

We left Disobey 2025 feeling inspired and energized. We are already looking forward to next year's event and the opportunities it will bring for hacking, learning, and community building.
