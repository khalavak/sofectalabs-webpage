            <p>
                In the evolving landscape of digital threats, Security Posture Management (SPM) has become a crucial component for organizations. SPM allows for the continuous assessment of security status, facilitating proactive responses to potential vulnerabilities.
            </p>

            <h3>The SPM Lifecycle</h3>
            <p>
                Understanding your organization's security posture involves evaluating existing protocols, identifying vulnerabilities, and taking steps to mitigate risk. This continuous cycle of <strong>assessment, improvement, and maintenance</strong> is critical in an environment where new threats emerge daily.
            </p>
