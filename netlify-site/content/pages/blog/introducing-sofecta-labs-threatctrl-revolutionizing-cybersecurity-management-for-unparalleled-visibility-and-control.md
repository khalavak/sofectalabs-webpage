            <h3>Granular Visibility</h3>
            <p>
                One of the key features of ThreatCTRL is its ability to provide granular visibility into the entire IT infrastructure. Whether on-premises or cloud-based, ThreatCTRL offers a unified view of assets, vulnerabilities, and potential risks. This holistic approach allows businesses to identify and prioritize security gaps effectively.
            </p>

            <h3>Proactive Governance</h3>
            <p>
                ThreatCTRL offers robust access controls, allowing administrators to define and enforce security policies across the network. By implementing proactive measures like manual threat hunting and incident response automation, businesses can significantly reduce detection time and minimize operational impact.
            </p>
