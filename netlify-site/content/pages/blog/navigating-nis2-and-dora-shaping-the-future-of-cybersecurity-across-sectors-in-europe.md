            <h3>Distinguishing NIS2 from DORA</h3>
            <p>
                NIS2 is a <strong>directive</strong>, requiring transposition into national law by October 2024. DORA is a <strong>regulation</strong>, immediately applicable across all member states by January 17, 2025. While NIS2 covers broad essential sectors, DORA focuses specifically on the financial sector's operational resilience.
            </p>

            <h3>Complementary Frameworks</h3>
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Supply Chain:</strong> NIS2 underscores the need for bolstered supply chain security.</li>
                <li><strong>Third-Party Risk:</strong> DORA emphasizes stringent management of third-party ICT providers.</li>
                <li><strong>Lex Specialis:</strong> For financial entities, DORA takes precedence as the specialized regulation.</li>
            </ul>
